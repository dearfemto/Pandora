1. Knowing your purpose

Everyone has a purpose in life, some people don't, and some people don't even know what that purpose is. There is three type of people:

  1. Those who believe in God know that the purpose of life is to worship God.
  2. Those who believe in God but do not fulfill their obligations also know that the purpose of life is to worship God.
  3. Those who do not have faith in God do not believe in the purpose of life.

These three types of people will always exist: from before you are born, while you are alive, and after you die. We live to worship God, but don't think the road will always be smooth when you cross it, because there will always be those who block your path. Everyone has their own way, their own dreams, and their own goals while on this earth. But remember, when you think about what your dreams are for the future, always consider what God likes and stay away from things that God doesn't like or forbids. Then you will get what you deserve when you die.

2. Patient is priority

Open your eyes wide, embrace clarity, and maintain your calm. This is the pathway to the success you seek. When serenity replaces panic, everything will fall into place. Your mind will find peace, and your body will thrive in calmness.

3. Always ask "WHY"

Why is this happening? Why am I here? Why am i doing this? Always seek the answers to these questions, for in your relentless pursuit, you will uncover the clues that guide you toward your true desires.

Mailure is a natural thing that happens and is experienced by everyone. Though I may not know you personally, I firmly believe that in my perspective, failure is not an option.
